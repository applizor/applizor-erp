## 🧪 Tailored Industry Verticals (erp.applizor.com)
Our Cloud ERP engine provides configurations tailored for specific storefront configurations:

### 1. Pharmacy Management (Pharma ERP)
- **Target:** Retail pharmacies, clinical chemist counters, and multi-location pharmaceutical networks.
- **Key Capabilities:**
  - *Expiry Alerts:* Automatic expiration date tracking by batch codes.
  - *Generic Drug Lookup:* Compound mapping to search alternative brand variations.
  - *Doctor Prescription Mapping:* Binding physician credentials with patient billing records.
  - *H1 Schedule Compliance:* Strict ledger tracking for regulated drug transactions.

### 2. Supermarket & Grocery
- **Target:** Supermarkets, organic food stores, and bulk grocery suppliers.
- **Key Capabilities:**
  - *Weighing Scale POS Integration:* Auto-parsing item barcode weights for fresh produce.
  - *Shelf & Rack Location Mapping:* Grid mapping for inventory locations (aisle/section).
  - *Bulk Price Updates:* Rapid spreadsheet catalog syncing.

### 3. Electronics & Appliances
- **Target:** High-value appliance retailers, mobile handset dealers, and hardware vendors.
- **Key Capabilities:**
  - *Serial/IMEI Tracking:* Individual unit tracking for serialized items.
  - *Warranty Registration:* Auto-generation of PDF warranty certificates.
  - *Product Attributes:* Product variant configurations (RAM, storage, color).

### 4. Departmental & Retail Chains
- **Target:** Apparel outlets, gift shops, and multi-branch retail franchises.
- **Key Capabilities:**
  - *Multi-Store Transfers:* Audited internal stock transfers between warehouses.
  - *Custom Barcode Label Generation:* Barcode designer for unbranded bulk merchandise.
  - *Distributor/Supplier Ledgers:* Vendor ledger tracking.

## 📦 Universal Enterprise Engine Capabilities
Universal modules running under a single, unified database ecosystem:

### 1. Point of Sale (POS v2)
- High-speed billing terminal built for rapid checkouts.
- Smart barcode scanning, auto-parsing, and quick-keys/shortcuts.
- Hold/Retrieve invoice queue management.
- **Offline Billing Resilience:** Automatically caches POS billing invoices locally in a database cache and syncs to cloud servers when connection returns.

### 2. Inventory & Warehousing
- Expiry triggers, batch tracking, rack/shelf mapping.
- Global stock explorer for multi-store visibility.
- Auto stock-replenishment notifications.

### 3. B2B Wholesale Network

### 4. Loyalty & CRM Campaigns

### 5. AI Intelligence Hub
